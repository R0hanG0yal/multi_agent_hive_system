How does the AI remember things?

Hive Assistant uses an advanced vector semantic search engine and a rolling context window to safely and securely remember things you tell it, like your name, preferences, or tasks!

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your memories are currently cached locally in the environment. All data operates under strict boundaries defined by your autonomous system deployment.

How do I link my Google account?

Go to the Login page and click 'Sign in with Google'. This will synchronize your persistent memory banks across all your devices once the production database is connected.
